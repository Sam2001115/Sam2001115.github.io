HARDWARE_LAYER

To overcome the low stall torque (15 g·cm) of the DC motors, I engineered a 5:1 gear reduction system. This multiplied the effective torque to 75 g·cm, providing the necessary force to move the 3D-printed chassis and dust chamber.

INTELLIGENCE_LAYER

The system operates on an ESP32 DevKit V1. To protect the microcontroller from the 5V Echo signals of the HC-SR04 sensors, I implemented a voltage divider circuit to step the signal down to a safe 3.3V.
